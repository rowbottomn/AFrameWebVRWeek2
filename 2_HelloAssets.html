Adding Assets to AFrame Projects

Last week we made some cool projects in AFrame. But if you want to really make amazing things, you will need to learn how to let Aframe know about larger, more complicated items you want to use in your scenes. They might be 3D models, music, video or textures.
Go to this site to find 360 images that we can use for our skybox. Be careful to not get too big an image Select one and drag it into your repl as I show in the video below. Protip...click fullscreen on video.

Once you have a large image, it might take a awhile for someone's browser to load the image or other media up. It would look awkward if some things loaded up but others did not. For this reason, we are going to preload and cache all assets for the page in a special asset section.
